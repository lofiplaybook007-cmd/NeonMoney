Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about NeonMoney, coins, deals, and rewards.

💰 About Coins
What are coins? +
Coins are NeonMoney's reward currency. You earn them by purchasing products through our affiliate links, spinning the daily wheel, maintaining daily streaks, and referring friends. 100 coins = ₹10 in real value, redeemable for gift cards, recharges, and exclusive deals.
How do I earn coins? +
You can earn coins in several ways:

🛒 Shopping: Click our affiliate links and purchase — coins are added based on the product's commission value.
🎰 Daily Spin: Spin the wheel once per day to win 5–100 coins.
🔥 Streak Bonus: Log in and spin daily for consecutive days to earn milestone bonuses.
👥 Referral: Invite friends — earn 200 coins per successful referral.
Why are my coins showing as "Pending"? +
When you purchase a product through our affiliate link, coins go into Pending status first. This is because we only receive the affiliate commission after the return/refund window (typically 15–30 days) closes on Amazon or Flipkart. Once the platform confirms the sale, your coins automatically move to Confirmed and become redeemable. Coins from spin and streaks are confirmed immediately.
When do coins expire? +
Confirmed coins are valid for 12 months from the date they are confirmed. Pending coins expire if the underlying purchase is returned or cancelled. You'll receive a notification 30 days before any coins are set to expire so you have time to redeem them.
How do I use / redeem coins? +
Go to the Rewards page, choose a reward (gift card, recharge, etc.), and click Redeem. You need a minimum of 500 confirmed coins to redeem. The voucher code will be sent to your registered email within 24–48 hours. Recharges are processed within 2–4 hours.
🔥 About Deals
How does price comparison work? +
We aggregate publicly available product pricing from Amazon India and Flipkart and display them side by side. We update prices regularly, but for the most accurate real-time price, always click through to the store to confirm before purchasing. Prices can change frequently due to flash sales and limited-time offers.
Are the prices always accurate? +
We strive to keep prices as accurate as possible, but we are not Amazon or Flipkart. Prices shown are for informational purposes. Always confirm the final price on the store's checkout page before completing a purchase. We are not responsible for price discrepancies.
What are Price Drop Alerts? +
Price Drop Alerts notify you when a product on your wishlist drops in price. You can set a target price, and we'll notify you via email or in-app notification when the deal matches your target. This helps you buy at the right time and avoid overpaying.
🔐 Account & Privacy
Is NeonMoney free to use? +
Yes! NeonMoney is completely free for users. We earn a small affiliate commission when you buy through our links. This commission does not increase the product price for you — you pay the same price as on the store directly. In fact, we use part of that commission to fund your coin rewards!
Is my data safe? Do you sell it? +
We do not sell your personal data. We collect only your email address and usage data (which products you view) to improve our service and track your rewards. Your data is stored securely on Firebase. Please read our full Privacy Policy for complete details.
Is NeonMoney directly affiliated with Amazon or Flipkart? +
No. NeonMoney is an independent affiliate platform. We are part of the Amazon Associates and Flipkart Affiliate programs, which are public programs open to any website. We earn a commission from qualifying purchases made through our links, but we are not directly affiliated with, endorsed by, or a subsidiary of Amazon or Flipkart. See our Disclaimer for full details.
