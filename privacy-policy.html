Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025 · Effective immediately

✅ We do not sell your personal data. Ever.

NeonMoney ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it.

1. What Data We Collect

  • Email address — provided when you create an account or sign up with Google
  • Name — provided during registration
  • Usage data — pages visited, products searched, deals clicked, and time spent on the platform
  • Device & browser information — for security and performance optimization
  • Affiliate click data — which links you click, to track coin rewards

2. Why We Collect This Data

  • Account management — to create and maintain your account
  • Reward tracking — to accurately track and assign coin rewards to your account
  • Service improvement — to understand what features are most useful
  • Price alerts — to notify you of price drops on products you're interested in
  • Communication — to send important account updates and reward notifications

3. How We Store & Protect Your Data

Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google's cloud platform), which complies with industry-standard security practices. We use encryption, secure connections (HTTPS), and access controls to protect your information.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share limited data only with:

  • Firebase / Google — our cloud infrastructure provider
  • Affiliate networks — anonymized click data to track commissions (no personal data)
  • Legal authorities — only if required by law

5. Cookies

We use cookies to maintain your login session and preferences. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work correctly.

6. Your Rights

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of marketing emails at any time

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions, email us at: privacy@neonmoney.in
