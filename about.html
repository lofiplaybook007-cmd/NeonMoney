Why NeonMoney Exists

Every day, millions of Indians overpay for products online — not because the best price doesn't exist, but because finding it takes too much time and effort. Amazon and Flipkart both run simultaneous deals, flash sales, and price cuts. The best price switches between them constantly.

NeonMoney was built to solve this in seconds. Compare, save, and get rewarded — all in one place.

We also believe you should be rewarded for smart shopping. That's why every purchase through our platform earns you coins you can redeem for real rewards.

The NeonMoney Story

It started with a simple frustration: buying headphones online. The same product was ₹1,299 on Amazon and ₹999 on Flipkart — but which site to check first? And by the time you finished checking, the flash sale had ended.

"Why doesn't a single place show me both prices instantly — and actually reward me for buying smart?"

That question led to NeonMoney. We built a platform that does exactly that: real-time price comparison between Amazon and Flipkart, with a coin rewards system layered on top so that every smart purchase becomes a double win.

We're a small, passionate team based in India. We understand the Indian shopping ecosystem — the festival sales, the flash deals, the price tricks. And we're here to make sure you never get caught on the wrong side of a price difference again.

We're just getting started. Product categories, more stores, and smarter alerts are all on the roadmap. Join us early — the rewards are better now than they'll ever be.
